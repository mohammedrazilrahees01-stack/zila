===================================================
  QR CODE SETUP — READ THIS BEFORE GOING LIVE
===================================================

STEP 1: Open your UPI app (Google Pay / PhonePe / Paytm)
STEP 2: Go to "Receive Money" > "Show QR Code"
STEP 3: Take a screenshot or download the QR image
STEP 4: Rename the file to:  qr.png
STEP 5: Replace this file by uploading your qr.png
         to this folder:  public/images/qr.png

The checkout page will automatically display your QR!
